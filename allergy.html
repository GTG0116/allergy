Health Tips for Allergy Sufferers

  • •

    Stay indoors during peak pollen hours (typically midday and early morning).

  • •

    Keep windows closed at home and in your car to limit exposure to outdoor allergens.

  • •

    Shower and change clothes after spending extended time outside.

  • •

    Use a HEPA filter in your home to clean the air of allergens.
